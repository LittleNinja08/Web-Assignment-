Why Enniscorthy ?

Here at the town of Eniscorthy located in Co.Wexford we welcome one and all to our beautiful town!

Located just 25 minuets from Wexford town and Gorey, Enniscorthy is full of rich and exciting history.

Enniscorthy is a place of beauty, with the river slaney running through the heart of the town. Just at the summit of vinnigar hill there is plenty for all the family to do. From the battle of the saxons to a quite evening for you and that special someone. There is something for everyone
